All software developers have to deal with errors in their code. The process of finding, fixing, and verifying bugs can be very time consuming. Of course, we all know that time means money. That's where tERROR can help. The tERROR service can reduce the time developers have to spend tracking and fixing errors. By automating many of the processes involved in error tracking, tERROR allows developers to spend more of their time producing software. tERROR integrates many necessary developer tools such as revision control, bug tracking, build systems, and testing to make sure that developers can get the most out of the available tools.

We have realized that it is very rare to have a truly unique error. That means that most developers spend time fixing problems that have already solved in another piece of software. Wouldn't it be nice if there was a way that developers could stop reinventing the wheel when it came to fixing bugs? That's why tERROR includes a statistic analysis module that can suggest known solutions to errors that have been fixed in the past. There are several tERROR subscription tiers available. The Community subscription level stores all known solutions in a public database. Private subscribers can choose to keep solutions annonymous. Corporate subscribers have their own private database.
